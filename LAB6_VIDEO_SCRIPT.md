### [0-5초] Introduction
Hi, I'll be presenting my D3 quantitative data visualizations for Lab 6.

### [5-15초] Present Summary Statistics
First, here are the codebase analysis results. Based on CSV data generated by elocuent, I'm showing statistics like total commits, lines, and files. Lines by file type are also shown with percentages.

### [15-30초] Demonstrate Scatterplot
Now, here's the scatterplot. X-axis shows dates, Y-axis shows time of day. Dot sizes are proportional to the number of lines committed. [Hover over a point] Hovering over points shows commit details in a tooltip.

### [30-45초] Demonstrate Brushing
[Drag to select area] You can drag to select multiple commits. Selected commits show count and language breakdown with percentages.

### [45-50초] What I Learned
The most challenging part of this lab was implementing the brushing feature. Specifically, I had to solve the issue where D3's brush overlay intercepted all mouse events, preventing tooltips from working. I used the raise() method to move dots above the overlay. Also, I had to declare xScale and yScale as global variables to access them in the brushing function due to scope issues. This helped me understand the conversion between SVG coordinate system and data coordinate system.
